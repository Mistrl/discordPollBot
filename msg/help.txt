**Commands:**
```/poll MyPoll,AltA,AltB,AltC``` *Creates a poll named MyPoll with the options AltA, AltB and AltC. To add more options, just keep adding comma-separated arguments*
```/vote 5``` *Votes for the 5th option*
```/help``` *Sends help message, duh...*
```/voters``` *Sends list of voters if allowed*
